Our Story

Tirshana Cake Bakery was founded in 2078 with a simple mission: to create extraordinary cakes that bring joy to every celebration. What began as a small home bakery has blossomed into a beloved local institution.

We believe that cakes are more than just desserts - they're the centerpiece of life's sweetest moments. That's why we pour our hearts into every creation.
